Biography

My name is Mehmet Gökgül, and I am a 22-year-old undergraduate Computer Engineering student currently living in Ankara. I completed my high school education at Aydınlıkevler Anadolu High School, which is one of the well-known high schools in the city. Initially, I enrolled in Software Engineering at Atılım University, but after some time, I transferred to Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University to pursue my passion for Computer Engineering. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong interest in fields like the defense industry and database administration. In the future, I aim to work in one of these fields, contributing to technological advancements and innovations.
